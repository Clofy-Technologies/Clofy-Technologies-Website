Personal Branding Score (PBS)

Unlock the Power of Your Personal Brand. Your PBS is more than just a number—it’s the key to standing out and thriving in today's digital world.

What is the Personal Branding Score (PBS)?

The Personal Branding Score (PBS) is an AI-driven tool that evaluates your digital footprint across platforms like LinkedIn, personal websites, and social media. It provides a score from 0 to 100 that reflects the strength of your personal brand and visibility.

The PBS is designed to:
- Help you assess how you’re perceived by potential employers, clients, or collaborators.
- Offer insights into areas for improvement in your personal brand.
- Guide your actions toward improving your online presence, network, and influence.

How is Your PBS Calculated?

Your PBS is calculated using a combination of factors that YAIGA’s advanced AI algorithms analyze, including:

  • LinkedIn Profile: Completion, professionalism, and endorsements.
  • Social Media Presence: Activity, alignment with brand message, professional tone.
  • Content & Engagement: Posting/sharing relevant content, engagement with your network.
  • Professional Portfolio: Quality of personal website, portfolio items, or case studies.
  • Reputation & Visibility: Mentions in industry discussions, articles, or publications.

Based on these factors, YAIGA generates a detailed report with your PBS (0–100), key strengths, improvement areas, and actionable recommendations.

Why is PBS Important?

Your PBS serves as a mirror to your online presence, offering a clear view of how effectively you’re positioning yourself for your goals. Here’s why it matters:

  • Stand Out in a Competitive Market: A strong personal brand increases visibility and credibility.
  • Identify Gaps: Understand what's working and what isn't in your online footprint.
  • Build Trust & Authority: A higher PBS indicates a professional, consistent presence.
  • Track Your Progress: Regular PBS updates let you monitor improvements over time.

How to Improve Your PBS

  • 1. Optimize Your LinkedIn Profile

    Complete every section (experience, education, skills), craft a compelling summary, and gather recommendations.

  • 2. Build a Consistent Online Presence

    Engage with your network, share relevant articles, and post content that aligns with your goals.

  • 3. Create a Personal Website or Portfolio

    Showcase your work with case studies, achievements, and easy contact info for potential collaborators.

  • 4. Grow Your Network

    Interact with industry leaders, attend events, and actively participate in communities aligned with your field.

  • 5. Monitor Your Digital Reputation

    Google yourself, clean up outdated or irrelevant info, and ensure your profiles remain professional.

Track Your PBS Progress

YAIGA allows you to track your PBS over time. Once you have your initial score, revisit your profile and see how your actions influence your brand. Each improvement yields new insights and updated recommendations, guiding you toward greater success in landing that dream job, building a powerful network, or growing your business.

FAQs

Q1: How often should I check my PBS?

We recommend checking your PBS every 1-3 months to track your progress and adjust your strategies as needed.

Q2: Can I improve my PBS without being active on social media?

Yes, while social media is a key factor, you can still improve your PBS by optimizing LinkedIn, building a strong portfolio, and engaging in offline networking opportunities.

Q3: Is my PBS shared with anyone?

Your PBS is personal and confidential. YAIGA provides recommendations for improvement, and these are never shared unless you choose to do so.
